## Getting Started

### Creating Your Profile

When you first use FinFind, we'll ask you a few questions to understand your financial goals:

1. **Investment Experience**: Are you a beginner, intermediate, or experienced investor?
2. **Risk Tolerance**: How comfortable are you with market fluctuations?
3. **Financial Goals**: What are you trying to achieve? (retirement, emergency fund, growth, etc.)
4. **Budget Range**: What's your comfortable investment range?

> üí° **Tip**: Be honest about your experience and risk tolerance. This helps us provide better recommendations!

### Understanding the Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Recent Searches**: Quick access to your search history
- **Recommendations**: Products we think match your profile
- **Saved Products**: Products you've bookmarked for later
- **Learning Progress**: How the system is adapting to your preferences

## Understanding Recommendations

### How Recommendations Work

Our AI considers multiple factors:

1. **Your Profile**: Risk tolerance, goals, experience
2. **Search History**: What you've looked for before
3. **Interactions**: Products you've viewed, saved, or rated
4. **Market Context**: Current rates, trends, and availability

### Match Scores

Each recommendation shows a match score:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 90-100% | Excellent match for your profile |
| 70-89% | Good match with minor differences |
| 50-69% | Partial match, review carefully |
| Below 50% | Alternative option to consider |

### Understanding Explanations

Click "Why this recommendation?" to see:

- **Key Match Factors**: What makes this product right for you
- **Potential Concerns**: Things to consider
- **Comparison Points**: How it compares to alternatives

## Comparing Products

### Side-by-Side Comparison

1. Select products to compare (up to 4)
2. Click "Compare Selected"
3. View features side by side

### What to Compare

| Aspect | What to Look For |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Fees** | Monthly fees, transaction fees, hidden costs |
| **Returns** | Interest rates, dividend yields, historical performance |
| **Requirements** | Minimum balance, credit score, income requirements |
| **Features** | Mobile app, customer service, additional perks |
| **Flexibility** | Withdrawal limits, lock-up periods, penalties |

### Finding Alternatives

On any product page, see:
- **Similar Products**: Same category, comparable features
- **Budget Options**: Lower cost alternatives
- **Premium Options**: Higher-tier alternatives with more features

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Beginners

1. **Start with your goals**: Know what you're trying to achieve
2. **Begin conservatively**: You can always increase risk later
3. **Read explanations**: Understand why products are recommended
4. **Ask questions**: Use natural language to explore

### For Research

1. **Compare multiple options**: Don't settle for the first result
2. **Check the details**: Read full product descriptions
3. **Consider fees**: Low returns can be eaten by high fees
4. **Look at alternatives**: The best option might not be obvious

### For Security

1. **Protect your profile**: Don't share account details
2. **Verify externally**: Always confirm details with the provider
3. **Stay updated**: Financial products change frequently

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is FinFind free to use?**
A: Yes, basic features are free. Premium features may require a subscription.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: We may receive referral fees from product providers, but this doesn't affect our recommendations.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal data with third parties.

### Search Questions

**Q: Why don't I see certain products?**
A: We only show products available in your region and suitable for your profile. Try adjusting filters or updating your profile.

**Q: Can I search for specific companies?**
A: Yes, include the company name in your search, e.g., "Vanguard ETFs under $100"

**Q: Why are results different each time?**
A: Our AI learns from your interactions, so recommendations improve over time.

### Recommendation Questions

**Q: Why was this product recommended to me?**
A: Click "Why this recommendation?" for a detailed explanation of the match factors.

**Q: Can I get more recommendations?**
A: Yes, scroll to the bottom of recommendations and click "Load More"

**Q: How often are recommendations updated?**
A: Recommendations update in real-time based on your activity and market changes.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions)

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Currently, FinFind is a web application optimized for mobile browsers.

**Q: Can I access FinFind offline?**
A: Some features require an internet connection, but saved products are available offline.
